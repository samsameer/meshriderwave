# MeshRider Wave Android
## One-Page Executive Summary

**Date:** January 2026 | **Status:** Beta (90% Complete) | **Target GA:** Q2 2026

---

## What Is It?

**MeshRider Wave** is a tactical Push-to-Talk (PTT) app for Android that works with DoodleLabs MeshRider radios. It enables secure voice communication without cellular or internet infrastructure.

---

## Why It Matters

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Traditional PTT requires cellular/servers | Works completely offline on mesh |
| Competitors charge monthly subscriptions | One-time deployment, no recurring fees |
| Generic apps not optimized for mesh | Native integration with DoodleLabs radios |
| Security often an afterthought | Military-grade E2E encryption built-in |

## Key Features

| Feature | Value |
